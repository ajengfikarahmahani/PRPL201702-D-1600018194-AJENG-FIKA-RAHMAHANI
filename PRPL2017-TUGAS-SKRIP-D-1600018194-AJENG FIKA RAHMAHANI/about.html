ABOUT US


Java Culture Hotel is a luxury hotel located in the heart of business district. Our hotel is convenient to several interest place and have gorgeous views of Slamet Mountain. Java Culture Hotel features 169 Rooms, Deluxes, Suites, Cottages, 16 Meeting Rooms, Grand Ballroom, and Convention Center. We also have the largest parking area which carried up to 500 cars, the biggest and the best Lagoon/Pool in Central Java with 96 metre wide. The Hotel also surrounded by central of entertainment such as Marcopolo Spa, Fitness Center, Aerobic Studio, and Club&KTV (Cheers! Never Ending Party).

As the largest hotel in town, we provide a variety of authentic food and beverage outlets. We also have have a rooftop, which can be a perfect place to get a nice view of the city. Our luxurious facilities which mixed with Javanese culture will generate an unforgettable experience.

For yout MICE needed, we provide you a Krisna Ballroom which can acomodate up to 850 people. In other side we also have Hastinapura Convention Centre, the largest venue which can acomodate up to 2000 people. We also have a meeting building which consist of 16 meeting rooms with a complete meeting facilities and equipments.
